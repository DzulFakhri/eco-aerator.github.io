About ECO AERATOR
FISHING AND TECHNOLOGY

This idea began when we encountered complaints expressed by shrimp farmers around the ITS campus. They complained that many shrimp died before they were ready to be harvested due to the lack of dissolved oxygen in the ponds. They need an aerator to maintain dissolved oxygen levels in the pond. However, problems arose again because the conventional aerators used by shrimp farmers require high operational costs, thereby reducing their income and productivity. We conducted a survey of shrimp farmers in several other areas in East Java, such as Lamongan and Gresik. The survey results that we get converge to a conclusion, that the problem lies with conventional aerators which require quite expensive operational costs and cause pollution to the environment. Therefore, we made a business innovation in the form of Eco-Aerator products based on environmentally friendly renewable energy sources to replace the use of conventional aerators among shrimp pond farmers. By utilizing technology based on renewable energy sources, the use of the Eco-Aerator only requires very cheap operational costs, does not pollute the air and the environment, and has a relatively longer tool life compared to conventional aerators. With the Eco-Aerator, shrimp farm farmers can maintain dissolved oxygen levels in the pond without worrying about operational costs. In addition, the Eco Aerator is equipped with sensors that are able to maintain oxygen levels and increase the productivity of pond farmers.
Target Customer:
B2C (Pond farmers in Indonesia)

Eco Aerator products

The Eco-Aerator product is an environmentally friendly aerator design based on renewable energy sources used to supply dissolved oxygen to shrimp ponds. The renewable energy source used by the Eco-Aerator is solar power (through solar / PV panel technology), considering that it has considerable potential in ponds and coastal areas. To anticipate changing weather conditions, the Eco-Aerator is equipped with an SCC (Solar charge controller) and a battery which is used to store the electrical energy that has been obtained. Furthermore, the electrical energy obtained will be used to move the paddle wheel to supply oxygen to the shrimp pond. In addition, the Eco-Aerator is also equipped with 2 (two) additional features to make it easier and more efficient to use by shrimp farming farmers. The first feature is the pH, Temperature, salinity, and dissolved DO sensors that are connected to the microcontroller system on the Eco-Aerator. This feature allows the Eco-Aerator to work automatically when the dissolved oxygen level is less than the required standard and can also stop automatically when the dissolved oxygen level is sufficient for the needs of the pond. The second feature is a wireless communication device. This feature allows users, in this case the pond farmers, to remotely control and monitor the Eco-Aerator. In addition, if there is trouble with the Eco-Aerator, pond farmers can easily contact technicians and developers for the troubleshooting process.

Value Propositions of Product (Keunggulan Produk):

- Maintaining optimal dissolved oxygen levels: Eco-Aerator uses a renewable energy source and is equipped with an automation system based on a dissolved oxygen sensor in shrimp ponds. This makes it easy for pond farmers to manage the Eco-Aerator operating schedule and no electrical energy is wasted.
- Low operating costs: Eco-Aerator uses PV technology which have an impact on operating costs that have to be incurred by farm farmers. This is a special selling point for pond farmers considering that they have complained about the high operational costs.
- Environmentally friendly: Pollution to the environment and air generated by conventional aerators will not occur in Eco-Aerators. Shrimp farming farmers can support the environmental conservation movement by using an environmentally friendly Eco-Aerator.
- Increasing the social value of the company with environmental awareness: The company will get 2 values ​​at the same time by supporting the Eco-Aerator to become a grant or CSR program. First, the company will be considered to be concerned about supporting the development of MSMEs, especially the welfare of shrimp farmers in Indonesia. Second, the company will be considered to have played a part in environmental conservation by using renewable energy sources that are environmentally friendly to replace conventional energy sources.
- With this Eco aerator, it can reduce operating costs by 10-15% per month (reducing fuel costs)
